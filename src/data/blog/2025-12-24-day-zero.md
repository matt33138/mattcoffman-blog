Friday marks one year without caffeine. A simple decision made on December 26, 2024, kept for 363 days and counting.

Two days from now, I start another experiment: writing and publishing here, every single day.

The commitment isn't to quality. 

It's to showing up. To articulating thought. To being a participant rather than an observer.

Let's see what emerges.
